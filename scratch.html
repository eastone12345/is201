Costa Rica is a Spanish-speaking country in Central America. As Wikipedia states, "It has a population of around five million in a land area of 51,060 km2 (19,710 sq mi). An estimated 333,980 people live in the capital and largest city, San José, with around two million people in the surrounding metropolitan area."

Costa Rica border Nicaragua to the North and Panama to the South. It has quite a tropical climate and is known for its wildlife. Because of this, Costa Rica is a very popular vacation spot. Here are just a few of the breathtaking sights that are found there:

Tourism is a large part of the economy of Costa Rica. Some of the most popular tourist sites (according to www.govisitcostarica.com) include the following:

  1. Arenal Volcano National Park & La Fortuna
    • Volcano
    • Jungle
    • Hot Springs
    • Zip Lining
  2. Corcovado National Park & Drake Bay
  3. Tamarindo, Playa Langosta & Playa Grande
  4. Manuel Antonio & Manuel Antonio National Park
  5. Monteverde Cloud Forest Reserve
  6. Tortuguero National Park
  7. Santa Teresa & Mal Pais
  8. Matapalo
  9. Puerto Viejo & Cahuita National Park
  10. Nosara & Playa Guiones
